Map the real system, context lifecycle, and trust boundaries.

Verify endpoint determinism, normalization stability, and reproducibility.

Verify non-bypassable execution and verification primitives.

## Phase 6 — Flow Control & Phase Discipline Audit
Verify strict wait-state behavior, redirect continuity, and no phase leakage.

Break the system intentionally and inspect safety behavior.

Verify Redis/Postgres/Chroma mapping, provenance, and retrieval alignment.

## Phase 9 — Metrics, Failure Matrix, and Remediation Design
Produce scored evidence, exploit scenarios, fixes, and invariance contract.

## Phase 10 — Production Hardening Lock-In
Convert findings into enforced invariants, runtime checks, and regression suite.
